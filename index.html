About

Nethdima Private Limited is a private company providing multi-functional services including internet television and radio broadcasting. Founded in 2017 by Dinush Mangala in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka, Nethdima Private Limited brings you the channels Nethdima TV and Nethdima FM, offering quality entertainment, news, and more. Our mission is to deliver exceptional broadcasting with a focus on quality content and viewer satisfaction. We aim to become a leading platform for digital entertainment and information dissemination in Sri Lanka, offering unparalleled viewer experiences through innovative programming and cutting-edge technology.

Nethdima TV

Nethdima TV offers a diverse range of television content designed to provide an exceptional viewing experience. Our programming includes genres such as drama, comedy, documentaries, and news, catering to various interests and age groups. We strive to bring the best of local and international entertainment to our viewers, ensuring there is something for everyone.
