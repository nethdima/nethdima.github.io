About NPL

×

Nethdima Private Limited (NPL), founded by Dinush Mangala on December 21, 2017, in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka, is dedicated to providing a diverse range of online radio and TV channels. Our mission is to deliver exceptional broadcasting with a focus on quality content and viewer satisfaction. We aim to become a leading platform for digital entertainment and information dissemination in Sri Lanka, offering unparalleled viewer experiences through innovative programming and cutting-edge technology.

Nethdima TV

Nethdima TV offers a diverse range of television content aimed at providing an exceptional viewing experience. Our programming includes a variety of genres such as drama, comedy, documentaries, and news, catering to different audience interests and age groups. We strive to bring the best of local and international entertainment to our viewers, ensuring there is something for everyone.

Nethdima FM

Nethdima FM is a premier destination for music, news, and entertainment. With a focus on delivering high-quality audio content, Nethdima FM broadcasts a wide range of music genres including pop, rock, classical, and traditional Sri Lankan music. Stay informed with our timely news updates and enjoy engaging entertainment shows hosted by talented presenters. Join us for a listening experience that resonates with your passions and interests.
